"The City of Lights"

Located in the north-central part of France on the river Seine, Paris is the country's cosmopolitan capital. "The City of Love," as people call it, is said to be one of the most beautiful and romantic cities brimming with culture, art, fashion, food, and history. Home to some of the most popular iconic monuments and museums.

From taking a stroll through backstreets to watching the Eiffel tower all lit up, Paris caters to people of every age. People dancing their hearts out and singing their top notes along the river Seine gives Paris a laidback feel during evenings. A mix of architecture, art, culture, and music makes Paris a dream destination for many.
